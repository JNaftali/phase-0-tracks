Elevator
  If your current floor is the same as the floor you need to reach, you don't need to use the elevator so you're done!
  If the floor you're going to is higher than your current floor
    push the top button in front of the elevator doors
  otherwise
    push the bottom button in front of the elevator doors
  wait until the doors open
  walk forward into the elevator, then turn around and look at the buttons next to the door
  For each button
    If the number on this button is the same as the floor you want to go
      push the button, and stop looking at the buttons
    otherwise, look at the next button
  Look at the display above the door
  Wait until the number above the door is the same as the number of the floor you're trying to get to
  walk out of the elevator

  Phone book lookup
    For each page in the phone book
      look at the left-most column on the page
      For each column on the page
        look at the top of the column
        For each thing in the column
          If this is the thing you're looking for
            Take note of the number next to this thing, and you're done!
          otherwise
            if there is a thing below it
              move to the thing below it.
            otherwise
              move to the thing at the top of the column
              if there is a thing to the right of this
                move to the right
              otherwise
                turn the page
                look at the leftmost part of the page
